The motherboard

The motherboard is the backbone of what all computer components connect to. It is the spine of the computer, and without it, all of the other components would be useless, just like humans without one as well, we would not have a structure to hold us up.
The motherboard houses the CPU, RAM, GPU, and everything has to connect to it.
